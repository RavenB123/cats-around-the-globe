Welcome to the kittys who live in Cat Village! I took the train through the mountains, on the outskirts of Taipei to find this place. Worth it!

The village sits along the Keelung River outside of New Taipei City, Taiwan. After the mines closed and the town declined... hundreds of cats came to roam the village!

You'll see cat themed cafes, murals, and shops all celebrating the village's furry residents.
